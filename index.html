Violinist – Singer – Guitarist

Greetings & Welcome!

I am Valentine, a French singer, guitarist, and classically trained violinist, passionate about Celtic music and currently residing in Oxfordshire, England.

My musical journey began in Le Havre, France, where a love for traditional song blossomed into a professional path. This passion led me to five years of classical violin studies at the Conservatoire de musique de Nîmes, where I earned my Grade 8 ABRSM diploma. Now based in the UK, I share my love for music by teaching violin, performing my solo Celtic repertoire at various events, and continuing my classical violin studies.

Professional Violin Lessons

Based in Oxfordshire, I offer high-quality violin lessons at my home or online. With a background in classical performance, I create personalised and engaging lessons for beginners and intermediate players, incorporating the music you love to make learning both effective and fun.

I also offer dynamic online lessons for students aged 12 and up. Using tools like screen sharing and digital annotation, these sessions are a convenient and highly productive alternative to in-person instruction.
